Door2Go

A website for individuals with skills willing to provide services at doorstep at a nominal cost and users looking for services without having to travel long distances or pay a lot of money for a service. How it works: Skilled individuals willing to provide doorstep service at a nominal cost will register himself/herself on the website with the details of the skills they posses, their level of expertise with certifications, qualifications, etc, along with other details like their charge, distance they can travel and any other relevant details. Users looking for a certain service and not willing to travel or do not want to pay more going to professionals or professional institutes can register themselves and find the necessary service and suitable instructor or service provider and interact with them directly and avail the service at their doorstep. To validate the credibility, an option to leave feedback and rate the skilled individual on their performance will be provided to the users. We could also provide an option to the user to speak to the other users who already availed a certain service to get the feedback.
